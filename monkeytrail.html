About

Whether you choose to walk or ride, you will find the Monkey Trail very challenging. Take the 30-minute or 1-hour trail through the lush forest paths of the eastern zoo.

Be prepared to get dirty! This is a fully outdoor, active experience. Wear comfortable shoes and bring water.

Where to Find It
Monkey Trail is at the eastern side of the zoo.
